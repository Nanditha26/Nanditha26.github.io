Mapping Vehicle Crime in Greater Manchester

In this project, I take a look at how vehicle crime is distributed across Greater Manchester and identify potential hotspots. The project also takes a look at what environmental features and sociodemographic factors are associared with higher incidents of vehicle crime.

What are the different ways to map incidents of crime?

As a part of a bigger project that tries to understand the ways people interact with different kinds of maps, this project looks at 5 different ways to visualise crime on a map: Point maps, Rectangular binning maps, Hexagonal Binning maps, Kernel Density Estimate maps, GI-Star maps and Cluster or Bubble maps.
